Pet & House Sitting

Reliable care for your pets and home while you're away, extended hospital visits, vacations or work. Hiring a house sitter is an investment in the security and well-being of your home. By understanding the costs involved, you can make an informed decision, and travel stress free.


Ensuring the safety of your home and belongings is a top priority when hiring a house sitter. Many professional house sitters carry liability insurance and bonding to protect against any potential damage or theft that may occur while they're caring for your property. Although this may increase the overall cost, it provides you with valuable peace of mind. Don't hesitate to ask potential house sitters about their insurance coverage and bonding arrangements to guarantee adequate protection.
